A Little Sunshine on Every Plate

Founded in 2014 by Chef Lucía Vega, Solstice was born out of a simple belief: the best memories are made around a table bathed in golden light. We partner with Sunvale’s winemakers and cheesemakers so every bite tells the story of our valley.
